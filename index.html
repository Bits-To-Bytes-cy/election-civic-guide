About Civic Flow

Civic Flow is a free, open-source educational tool designed to demystify the election process. Whether you're a first-time voter or a seasoned participant, our step-by-step guide helps you understand every phase — from registering to vote, all the way through post-election civic engagement. Built with accessibility at its core, Civic Flow includes a high-contrast mode and responsive design to ensure everyone can access this vital information.
